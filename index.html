Wei Wang

Associate Professor @ CASIA • AI Safety

I completed my PhD in Pattern Recognition from the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA), where I was advised by Tieniu Tan during 2012-2017. I am currently an Associate Professor of the New Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), CASIA. I am a member of IEEE, CCF, CAAI, etc. I am also a member of technical executive committee on Digital Forensics and Security of CSIG, technical committee (TC) on Computer Vision of CCF, TC on Pattern Recognition of CAAI, etc. My current research interests include artificial intelligence and its security problem, image and video forensics and privacy, and information content security.
